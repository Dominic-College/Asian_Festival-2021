Asian Festival 2021

What is the Asian Festival?

The Asian Festival is a 3 day long event for the students in Kinder to Grade 6. Students in the years participate in a range of hands-on and immersive experiences and activites.

Why do we have the Asian Festival?

The reason we have the Asian Festival is to deepen our understanding of the diverse cultures of Asia and the countries in Asia, we also have the Asian Festival to develop our knowledge and gain more knowledge about our close neighbours.

When is the Asian Festival running?

This year the Asian Festival will be starting on the 25th of October and will be concluding on the 27th of October, on the final day of the Asian Festival there will be an assembly taking place to celebrate the different countries and cultures of Asia as a whole.

What is Happening during the Asian Festival

During the Asian Festival this year students will be participating in a range of different activites. Activities that have happened in past years are classes have all participated in creating a whole artwork that other grades come and look at, some cooking classes have made Asian foods to share with other grades, student's parents have come in and shared with other students about their Asian backgrounds or heritage and finally, some optional activities run during recess and lunch times for students to participate in.
