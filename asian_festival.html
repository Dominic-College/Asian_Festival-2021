Asian Festival 2021

What is the Asian Festival?

The Asian Festival is a three day long event at Dominic College for the students in Kinder to Grade 6. Students in the years participate in a wide range of hands-on, immersive experiences and activites throughout the school on these three days.

Why do we have the Asian Festival at Dominic College?

At Dominic College we have the Asian Festival so we have a chance to celebrate all of the different cultures of Asia as a whole school, the other reasons for having the Asian Festival is so we can deepen our understanding of the diverse cultures in Asia and to foster knowledge about our close neighbour.

When is the Asian Festival running?

This year the Asian Festival will be taking place in week 3 starting on the 25th of October and will be concluding on the 27th of October, on past Asian Festivals, we have had an assembly on the final day for all of our grades to show what they have been learning about or for students to show what they have learned about so far.

What is Happening during the Asian Festival

During the Asian Festival this year students will be participating in a range of different activites. Activities that have happened in past years are classes have all participated in creating a whole artwork that other grades come and look at, some cooking classes have made Asian foods to share with other grades, student's parents have come in and shared with other students about their Asian backgrounds or heritage and finally, some optional activities run during recess and lunch times for students to participate in.
